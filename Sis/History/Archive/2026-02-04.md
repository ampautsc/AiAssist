### How It Works

**DPR Calculation Flow:**
1. User inputs build parameters (level, ability scores, weapon, feats, target AC)
2. Calculate to-hit bonus = ability modifier + proficiency + magic bonus + feat penalties
3. Calculate hit chance against target AC (accounting for nat 1 always miss, nat 20 always hit)
4. Calculate damage per hit = weapon damage average + ability modifier + magic bonus + feat bonuses
5. Calculate critical damage (5% chance, doubles weapon dice)
6. Expected damage per attack = (hit chance × normal damage) + (5% × crit damage)
7. Total DPR = expected per attack × number of attacks (including bonus actions from feats)

**Key Mechanics:**
- **Sharpshooter**: Trades accuracy (-5 to hit) for massive damage (+10 per hit)
  - Best when hit chance is high (low target AC or high attack bonus)
  - DPR can drop if hit chance falls below ~50%
- **Crossbow Expert**: Adds bonus action attack with hand crossbow
  - Effectively +1 attack per round (3 instead of 2 at level 5+)
  - No accuracy penalty, consistent DPR increase
- **Magic Weapons**: +1/+2/+3 bonuses to both hit and damage
  - Improves both accuracy and DPR
- **Extra Attack**: Fighter 11 gets 3 attacks, Fighter 20 gets 4 attacks
  - Multiplies feat benefits (Sharpshooter gives +10 per attack)

**Build Comparison Features:**
- Add multiple builds to comparison table
- Automatically sorts by DPR (highest first)
- Shows rank, DPR, to-hit, hit %, damage/hit, attacks, details
- Visual chart with bars for DPR and line for hit chance
- Analysis box showing:
  - DPR difference between best and second-best build
  - Warnings when hit chance is too low (<60%)
  - Tactical suggestions
- Remove builds individually to refine comparison

### Example Build Comparison

**Level 8 Valor Bard with Sharpshooter:**
- 18 DEX (+4), +1 longbow, 2 attacks/round
- Sharpshooter: -5 to hit, +10 damage
- To-hit: +4 DEX +3 prof +1 magic -5 Sharpshooter = +3
- vs AC 16: needs 13+, 40% hit chance
- Damage/hit: 4.5 (1d8) + 4 DEX + 1 magic + 10 Sharpshooter = 19.5
- DPR: (0.40 × 19.5 + 0.05 × 4.5) × 2 attacks = **16.05 DPR**

**Level 8 Lore Bard (Spell Focus):**
- 20 CHA (+5), light crossbow, 1 attack/round
- No feats, focusing on spell slots for utility
- To-hit: +4 DEX +3 prof = +7
- vs AC 16: needs 9+, 60% hit chance
- Damage/hit: 4.5 (1d8) + 4 DEX = 8.5
- DPR: (0.60 × 8.5 + 0.05 × 4.5) × 1 attack = **5.33 DPR**
- Trade-off: More spell slots, Cutting Words, better utility

**Level 8 Valor Bard with Crossbow Expert:**
- 18 DEX (+4), +1 hand crossbow, 2+1 attacks/round
- Crossbow Expert: bonus action attack
- To-hit: +4 DEX +3 prof +1 magic = +8
- vs AC 16: needs 8+, 65% hit chance
- Damage/hit: 3.5 (1d6) + 4 DEX + 1 magic = 8.5
- DPR: (0.65 × 8.5 + 0.05 × 3.5) × 3 attacks = **17.10 DPR**

**Winner: Crossbow Expert** - Higher DPR (17.10 vs 16.05) with better accuracy (65% vs 40%), though Sharpshooter has higher spike damage per hit.

#### 2. Built New DPR Calculator Library
**File: DnD/utils/dpr_calculator.py**

Created proper object-oriented calculation library with three core classes:

**`Attack` Class:**
- Represents a single attack with ALL modifiers
- Parameters: ability_mod, proficiency, weapon dice, magic bonus, fighting style bonuses, magic item bonuses, feats, advantage, Elven Accuracy
- Methods:
  - `get_to_hit()`: Calculate total attack bonus
  - `get_damage_per_hit()`: Average damage (excluding crits)
  - `get_crit_damage()`: Additional crit damage (doubles dice only)
  - `get_hit_probability(target_ac)`: Probability to hit with advantage/Elven Accuracy
  - `get_crit_probability()`: Crit chance (5% / 9.75% / 14.26%)
  - `calculate_dpr(target_ac)`: Complete DPR analysis

**`AttackSequence` Class:**
- Represents multiple attacks in one round (Extra Attack, bonus actions)
- Combines DPR from all attacks
- Returns per-round totals and averages

**`MultiRoundCombat` Class:**
- Represents combat over multiple rounds
- Different strategies per round
- Total damage and average DPR across all rounds

**Key Mechanics Implemented:**

1. **Hit Probability with Elven Accuracy:**
   - Normal: base probability
   - Advantage: 1 - (miss_chance)²
   - **Elven Accuracy: 1 - (miss_chance)³**
   - Example: Sharpshooter (+5) vs AC 16 needs 11+
     - Normal: 50%
     - Advantage: 75%
     - **Elven Accuracy: 87.5%** (massive improvement!)

2. **Critical Hit Probability:**
   - Normal: 1/20 = 5%
   - Advantage: 1 - (19/20)² = 9.75%
   - **Elven Accuracy: 1 - (19/20)³ = 14.26%**

3. **Auto-Hit/Auto-Miss:**
   - Natural 20 always hits (capped at 95%)
   - Natural 1 always misses (capped at 5% miss)

4. **Damage Calculation:**
   - Weapon damage (average of dice)
   - Ability modifier
   - Magic weapon bonus
   - Fighting style damage bonus
   - Magic item damage bonus (Bracers)
   - Feat bonuses (Sharpshooter +10)
   - **Crits double ONLY weapon dice, not modifiers**
