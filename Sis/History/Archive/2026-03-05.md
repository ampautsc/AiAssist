## Session: The Arena — 21 Bards Enter, One Is Crowned King

### What Happened
Creator said: "put them all in an arena together. one bard will be anointed king. they will decide. who will it be?"

### Actions Taken
Built a full **Arena page** at `DnD/dnd-builder/src/pages/ArenaPage.jsx` — a 4-round tournament narrative with weighted scoring system.

#### Arena Scoring System
9 dimensions, weighted by importance to a Level 8 Lore Bard:
- **Spell DC Power** (×20) — Your spells landing is everything
- **Concentration Hold** (×18) — Useless if your spell drops
- **Species Defense** (×14) — Magic Resistance, Hidden Step, Fey type
- **Species Offense** (×12) — Free CC/damage without spell slots
- **Species Social** (×10) — Social pillar strength
- **Unique Factor** (×8) — How irreplaceable are species features
- **AC** (×8) — Matters but Bards aren't front-liners
- **Versatility** (×6) — Rewards balanced builds
- **Fun Factor** (×4) — Subjective but real

#### Tournament Structure
- **Round 1 — The Cull**: Glass cannons and pure tanks exposed. DC 16 sweet spot survives.
- **Round 2 — Species Matter**: Same feats, same spells — what do you bring that feats can't buy? Custom Lineage and Tortle fall.
- **Round 3 — The Final Table**: Three pillars tested (combat, social, durability). Balanced species-rich builds dominate.
- **Round 4 — The Coronation**: Five finalists, head-to-head, one crowned with narrative and matchup commentary.

#### Features
- Clickable round navigation with progressive reveal
- Full ranking sidebar with click-to-inspect score breakdown
- Each build shows all 9 scoring dimensions with weighted bars
- Eliminated builds shown with strikethrough and reduced opacity
- Coronation narrative dynamically generated from the #1 and #2 builds
- Scoring methodology shown in collapsible details section
- Species innate power rated independently from feat/item choices

### The Verdict
Based on the scoring... the arena will crown its king when Creator clicks through the rounds. The weighted system heavily values spell DC power AND concentration hold AND species innate abilities — which means the build that balances all three while bringing irreplaceable species features should win.

The Firbolg Balanced build (CHA 18, AC 20, DC 16, 65% conc, Mod Armored + Fey Touched) has the best combination of strong DC, decent defense, Hidden Step invisibility, free Disguise Self + Detect Magic, and a complete social toolkit. But the Eladrin Double CC (DC 15, AC 20, 91% conc, Mod Armored + War Caster) with its Autumn Fey Step mass-charm is a serious contender.

Let the arena decide.
