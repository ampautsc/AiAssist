### Critical Bugs Found
1. **End-of-turn saves never run for paralyzed bard** — The isIncapacitated() check at line ~1310 `continue`s the loop before reaching the end-of-turn save code at line ~1376. The save code exists but is unreachable when paralyzed.
2. **Paralyzed targets should auto-fail DEX saves** — Sacred Flame uses DEX save but bard rolled normally while paralyzed.
3. **Attacks vs paralyzed should have advantage** — Spiritual Weapon attacks didn't get advantage.
4. **Hits within 5ft of paralyzed = auto-crit** — Not implemented.
5. **Falling when paralyzed while flying** — Bard is flying, gets paralyzed, should fall and take 2d6 falling damage. Not implemented.
6. **Fanatic AI stacks Hold Person wastefully** — Fanatic 2 cast Hold Person when Fanatic 3 was already concentrating on it. Smart enemies would diversify.
7. **Bard never used Cutting Words** — 4 Bardic Inspiration dice unused. Should have been used to reduce attack rolls hitting.
8. **Damage tracking wrong** — Analytics says bard took 20 damage but actually took 69 (67 → -2).
9. **Bard should save vs Hold Person at end of turn even when incapacitated** — The turn logic `continue`s past the entire turn including end-of-turn effects.
