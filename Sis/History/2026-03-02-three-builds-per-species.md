# 2026-03-02 - Three Builds Per Species

## What Happened
Creator asked for THREE builds per finalist species instead of one locked-in build. "Don't lock in so hard." Each build gets 2 feats (3 for Custom Lineage) and 2 uncommon magic items.

## What Was Built
Added ~350 lines to LORE_BARDS_BY_SPECIES.txt covering:

### 7 Species × 3 Builds = 21 Total Builds
- **Build A** = Safe/Tank (AC 19-20, DC 15, Adv CON or double CON)
- **Build B** = Balanced (AC 19-20, DC 16, partial CON coverage)
- **Build C** = Glass Cannon (AC 14-15, DC 17, no CON, highest impact)

### Species Covered
1. **Firbolg** - Evasion/Balanced/Fortress. Hidden Step as concentration protection.
2. **Eladrin** - Double CC/High DC/Balanced. Fey Step + spell combos.
3. **Gem Dragonborn** - Tank/Dragon Fear/Max CHA. Dragon Fear + Hypnotic Pattern = double mass CC.
4. **Satyr** - Tank/Balanced/Max CHA. Magic Resistance carries glass cannon builds.
5. **Yuan-Ti** - Tank/Social Predator/Max CHA. Free Suggestion at DC 17 is devastating.
6. **Custom Lineage** - Full Package/Max CHA Armored/Triple Half-Feat. Only species with CHA 20 + AC 20.
7. **Tortle** - Double CON/Balanced/Max CHA. 99% concentration hold on Build A.

### Master Comparison Table
All 21 builds in one table: AC, CHA, DC, CON protection, signature trick.
Notable standouts section highlighting best-in-class for each dimension.

## Key Math Insight
CHA starts at 17 (odd) from point buy. This means:
- Two half-feats = CHA 19 (odd, WASTED) — avoided this trap
- ASI +2 + half-feat = CHA 20 (optimal for 2-feat species)
- Three half-feats = CHA 20 (Custom Lineage only)

## Decisions Made
- Gave each species builds representing genuinely different strategies, not just +/- 1 to stats
- Included specific uncommon items that synergize with each build's philosophy
- Dragon Fear on Gem Dragonborn is highlighted as "two simultaneous AoE CC" — this is unique
- Noted Tortle can dump DEX (natural armor ignores it)
- Yuan-Ti Build B gets Hat of Disguise to solve the appearance problem
- Custom Lineage B is the ONLY build in the doc with CHA 20 + AC 20
