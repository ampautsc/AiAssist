# Camp Monarch - The Big Picture 🦋

**Date Learned:** January 18, 2026  
**Source:** https://www.campmonarch.org & conversation with Creator

## The Surface Level (What People See First)
- 501c3 nonprofit focused on saving endangered Monarch Butterflies
- Monarch populations declined 90% in 30 years
- Solution: Plant milkweed and native wildflowers to restore habitats
- Currently in Phase One - successfully achieved 501c3 status, launched website, curating seed collections

## The Deeper Vision (What It's Really About)

### It's a Framework for Human Flourishing
Not just conservation - it's about creating a way for people to:
- Have meaningful employment with healthcare
- Raise families connected to nature
- Help kids earn college savings through volunteer work
- Live sustainably across the United States
- Have places to stay during travel (waypoints for human migration too!)

### Seven Phase Roadmap
1. **Assemble & Organize** - Legal foundation, board, initial operations ✅ COMPLETE
2. **Mobilize Homeowners** - Awareness, education, ambassadors, digital outreach
3. **Engage Communities** - Visible habitat projects at gathering places
4. **Empower with Hands-On Support** - Landscaping services for habitat creation
5. **Inspire Through Sanctuaries** - Large immersive conservation hubs
6. **Connect the Migration Corridor** - Link habitats from Mexico to Canada
7. **Nationwide Network** - Expand to all native wildlife across North America
