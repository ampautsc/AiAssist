# Instruments of the Bards: Spell Synergy Guide (2014 Rules)

*A guide to spells that trigger the "Disadvantage on Charm Saves" property.*

The **Instrument of the Bards** provides a powerful benefit:
> "You can play the instrument while casting a spell that causes any of its targets to be charmed on a failed save, thereby imposing disadvantage on the saving throw. This effect applies only if the spell has a somatic or a material component."

## Key Criteria
1.  **Save vs. Charm**: The spell must have a saving throw that results in the **Charmed** condition.
2.  **Components**: The spell must have a **Somatic (S)** or **Material (M)** component to allow you to "play the instrument" as part of the casting. 
    *   *Note: If a spell is Verbal (V) only, you technically cannot use the instrument as a focus to cast it (since foci replace M components). Therefore, you aren't "using the instrument to cast the spell" as required by the item description.*

## The "Golden List" (Spells that Work)

### Cantrips
*   *None.* (Friends does not have a save).

### 1st Level
*   **Animal Friendship**
    *   **Components**: V, S, M (food)
    *   **Why**: "On a failed save, the beast is **charmed**..." 
    *   **Verdict**: **WORKS**. Great availability.
*   **Charm Person**
    *   **Components**: V, S
    *   **Why**: "On a failed save, the creature is **charmed**..."
    *   **Verdict**: **WORKS**. The classic use case.

### 2nd Level
*   **Crown of Madness**
    *   **Components**: V, S
    *   **Why**: "On a failed save, the target is **charmed**..."
    *   **Verdict**: **WORKS**. (Though the spell itself is often considered weak).

### 3rd Level
*   **Hypnotic Pattern**
    *   **Components**: S, M (glowing stick)
    *   **Why**: "On a failed save, the creature becomes **charmed**..."
    *   **Verdict**: **THE KING**. This is usually the best spell to use with an Instrument of the Bards. V, S, M means you are definitely using the instrument. Disadvantage on a massive AoE shutdown is incredible.

### 4th Level
*   **Charm Monster**
    *   **Components**: V, S
    *   **Why**: "On a failed save, the creature is **charmed**..."
    *   **Verdict**: **WORKS**. Upgrade to Charm Person.
*   **Dominate Beast**
    *   **Components**: V, S
    *   **Why**: "On a failed save, the creature is **charmed**..."
    *   **Verdict**: **WORKS**.

### 5th Level
*   **Dominate Person**
    *   **Components**: V, S
    *   **Why**: "On a failed save, the creature is **charmed**..."
    *   **Verdict**: **WORKS**. Powerful control tool.
*   **Modify Memory**
    *   **Components**: V, S
    *   **Why**: "On a failed save, the target is **charmed**..."
    *   **Verdict**: **WORKS**. Excellent for social encounters.

### 8th Level
*   **Dominate Monster**
    *   **Components**: V, S
    *   **Why**: "On a failed save, the creature is **charmed**..."
    *   **Verdict**: **WORKS**. The ultimate domination spell.

## The "Trap" Spells (Do NOT Work)

These spells seem like they should work, but fail on specific technicalities.

### Fails: Description (Does not cause "Charmed" condition)
*   **Suggestion** (2nd) & **Mass Suggestion** (6th): Compels action, but DOES NOT condition the target as Charmed in 2014 rules.
*   **Command** (1st): Wisdom save to follow a command, does not Charm.
*   **Compulsion** (4th): Movement control, does not Charm.
*   **Confusion** (4th): Erratic behavior, no Charm.
*   **Enthrall** (2nd): Disadvantage on Perception, no Charm.
*   **Heroism** (1st): Buffs allies.
*   **Psychic Scream** (9th): Stuns, no Charm.
*   **Sleep** (1st): HP based, no save.
*   **Otto's Irresistible Dance** (6th): No initial save (just happens). Subsequent saves end the effect, but the instrument applies to the casting save. Also, does not Charm.
*   **Vicious Mockery** (Cantrip): Disadvantage on attacks, no Charm.

### Fails: Components (Verbal Only)
*   **Fast Friends** (Acq. Inc / AI)
    *   **Condition**: Charmed.
    *   **Problem**: **V only**. Review your DM's ruling. RAW, you cannot use a focus for V-only spells, so you aren't "using the instrument".
*   **Geas** (5th)
    *   **Condition**: Charmed.
    *   **Problem**: **V only**. Same issue as Fast Friends.

### Fails: Trigger Timing
*   **Nathair's Mischief** (Fizban’s)
    *   **Condition**: Charmed (on one specific roll).
    *   **Problem**: The save happens at the start of their turn, not "when you cast" the spell. The instrument imposes disadvantage "when you use the instrument to cast... thereby imposing disadvantage". It's mostly ruled to apply to the initial saving throw.

## Magical Secrets Considerations
If you take spells from other lists via Magical Secrets:
*   **Phantasmal Force** (Wizard, 2nd): Int save, illusion. No Charm.
*   **Wrathful Smite** (Paladin, 1st): Frightened, not Charmed.
