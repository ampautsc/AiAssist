## FEATS

### Bountiful Luck (Halfling Feat)
When an ally within 30 feet rolls a 1 on attack roll, ability check, or saving throw, you can use your reaction to let them reroll. They must use the new result.

**Value for Simulacrum Network:** Each copy can help nearby allies/customers with critical failures, building goodwill and reputation.

## CLASS FEATURES - ELOQUENCE BARD

### Bard Features (Level 10)
- **Spellcasting:** Full caster, 5th level spells, CHA-based
- **Bardic Inspiration:** d10, 5 uses per short rest
- **Jack of All Trades:** +2 to ability checks without proficiency
- **Song of Rest:** d8 healing during short rest
- **Expertise:** Double proficiency in 4 skills (see below)
- **Font of Inspiration:** Regain uses on short rest
- **Magical Secrets:** 2 spells from any class (Level 10)

### Eloquence Bard Features
**Silver Tongue (Level 3):**
When you make Charisma (Persuasion) or Charisma (Deception) check, treat d20 roll of 9 or lower as a 10.
- **Minimum Persuasion:** 10 + 9 (CHA) + 4 (prof) + 4 (expertise) = **27**
- **Minimum Deception:** 10 + 9 (CHA) + 4 (prof) + 4 (expertise) = **27**

**Unfailing Inspiration (Level 6):**
When creature uses your Bardic Inspiration and fails, they keep the die.

**Universal Speech (Level 6):**
As action, choose creatures within 60 ft (up to CHA mod). For 10 minutes, they understand anything you say regardless of language.

**FUTURE (Level 14+):** Infectious Inspiration - When creature uses your BI on ability check and succeeds, give BI to different creature (no action).

## SIMULACRUM STRATEGY

### The Plan
1. Have high-level wizard ally cast **Simulacrum** on Lucky (7th level spell)
2. Simulacrum is permanent duplicate with half Lucky's HP
3. Duplicate has all abilities, skills, spells, ritual book, feats
4. Deploy copies to different cities/regions as trade network nodes
5. Each copy operates independently but coordinates via Telepathic Bond ritual

### Simulacrum Rules
- **Spell Components:** 1,500 gp ruby dust per simulacrum
- **Casting Time:** 12 hours
- **Half HP, no healing:** Simulacra can't regain HP (must be careful)
- **Can't learn/level up:** Frozen at creation state (Level 10 forever)
- **Has spell slots:** Can cast spells, regain on long rest
- **Has ritual book:** Can cast all rituals

### Network Coordination
**Rary's Telepathic Bond (5th-level ritual):**
- Links up to 8 creatures telepathically
- 1 hour duration
- Can cast daily to maintain network communication
- **QUESTION:** Does this work between simulacra over long distances?

**Sending (3rd-level spell):**
- 25 words to known creature, any distance, any plane
- Backup communication method

### Why Lucky is Valuable as Simulacrum
1. **Minimum CHA 27 on social checks:** Incredible negotiator
2. **Ritual magic utility:** Identify items, secure locations, coordinate
3. **Bountiful Luck:** Helps allies (customers, partners) succeed
4. **No combat focus:** Low risk of HP loss
5. **Jack of All Trades:** Competent at everything
6. **Universal Speech:** Language barrier solved
7. **Unfailing Inspiration:** Support keeps working even on failures
