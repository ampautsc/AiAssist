# 🎭 The Redeemer — Changeling Eloquence Bard

*"I took everything from them. Then I convinced them it was their idea. And the beautiful part? By the time I was done talking... they actually believed they were better for it. Some of them even were."*

**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

---

## Character Concept

**High Concept:** A criminal who only targets other criminals — infiltrating their organizations using shapeshifting and silver-tongued deception, robbing them blind, then convincing them to abandon their evil ways. Part Robin Hood, part con artist, part unlikely evangelist. A Dexter who uses words instead of knives.

**The Tension:** He's not pretending to be good. He genuinely believes he's doing the right thing. But his methods are undeniably criminal — theft, deception, manipulation, memory alteration. The question that haunts him: *Can you do evil things for good reasons and still call yourself good?*

**Personality Traits:**
- Disarmingly charming — even when you know he's lying, you want to believe him
- Genuinely empathetic — he understands WHY criminals turn to crime, because he's one of them
- Meticulous planner who improvises brilliantly when plans fall apart
- Collects "success stories" — reformed criminals he's turned around — and keeps mental tallies

**Ideal:** *"Everyone deserves a second chance. Some people just need to be robbed first."*

**Bond:** A criminal mentor who raised him and whom he's never been able to reform — his greatest failure and deepest motivation.

**Flaw:** He enjoys the con too much. The rush of becoming someone else, of bending people to his will — sometimes he's not sure if he's saving people or just feeding his own need for control.

## Racial Features — Changeling

### Shapechanger (The Core Fantasy)
- **Action** to change appearance: physical features, height (±6"), weight, voice, coloring, hair, sex
- Reverts on death
- **NOT magical** — immune to Dispel Magic, Detect Magic, Antimagic Field
- Can replicate specific individuals (Deception check if scrutinized)
- **Duration:** Indefinite — no concentration, no time limit

> **Critical Synergy:** Unlike Disguise Self (illusion, detectable), Changeling shapeshifting is *physically real*. You actually become that person. The only counter is True Seeing or a contested Insight vs. Deception check.

### Changeling Instincts
- **Two skill proficiencies** from: Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Persuasion
- **Pick:** Insight + Persuasion (Deception comes from Criminal background)

### Other Traits
- **Type:** Fey (not humanoid — immune to Hold Person, Charm Person, etc. that target humanoids!)
- **Size:** Medium or Small (your choice — can change with Shapechanger)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Languages:** Common + 2 others

> ⚠️ **MotM Changeling is Fey type.** This is HUGE. Hold Person, Charm Person, Dominate Person — all explicitly target humanoids. You're immune to all of them. This is one of the most underrated defensive features in the game.

## Feat Progression

### Level 4: Telepathic ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (The Mentalist Grifter)

Since Eloquence Bard's "Silver Tongue" feature already ensures your minimum Deception roll is stratospheric (around a 20+ by level 8 without even trying), the Advantage granted by the *Actor* feat is mathematically entirely redundant. We don't need help rolling high. Instead, take this half-feat to round out your Charisma to 18 and dramatically expand your grifting toolkit:

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Source** | Tasha's Cauldron of Everything |
| **CHA Increase** | +1 (bringing you to 18) |

**Benefits:**
- **Silent Communication:** Speak telepathically to anyone within 60 feet. Perfect for coordinating a heist while smiling at the mark, or pulling the "Gaslight Grift" (telepathically threatening someone using a fake mental voice to make them think they are being targeted by unseen casters or a rival boss).
- **Component-less Mind Reading:** You get to cast *Detect Thoughts* once per day **without requiring material, verbal, or somatic components.** You can literally read a crime boss's surface thoughts while maintaining eye contact, and they won't even see you cast a spell.

*(Note: Choosing this over Fey Touched means we lack a natural teleport for escape. If grappled or cornered, you will have to rely on Polymorph [Level 4 spell] or raw Deception/Persuasion to get out).*

---

### Level 8: Ritual Caster (Wizard) OR +2 Charisma

- **Ritual Caster (Wizard):** Gain *Find Familiar* (Owl for Help action advantage engine, scouting) and *Comprehend Languages*. Utility god mode. You delay 20 CHA until 12, but gain a massive solution to range/scouting problems.
- **+2 Charisma (Alternative):** CHA 18 → **20** (+5 mod). Spikes your DC to 16. Pure brute-force upgrade for social checks and saves.

> **Why consider Ritual Caster?** Bards have terrible range and no reliable way to gain advantage for their party natively. An Owl familiar solves action economy issues, and rituals like *Tiny Hut* and *Identify* fit the criminal mastermind perfectly.

- If you took Ritual Caster at 8, max your CHA to 20 here.
- If you maxed CHA at 8, take Resilient (CON) to protect *Hypnotic Pattern* and *Greater Invisibility* concentrations.

### Eloquence Subclass Features

**Level 3 — Silver Tongue:**
- Persuasion and Deception checks: treat d20 rolls of 9 or lower as 10
- Minimum result of 29 at level 8+ with expertise and 20 CHA
- This is *always on* — no resources, no limit

**Level 3 — Unsettling Words:**
- **Bonus action:** Expend Bardic Inspiration die
- Target within 60 ft subtracts the die from its **next saving throw**
- This is the bounded-accuracy-breaking ability — effectively +3.5 to +6.5 to your spell save DC
- At level 10 with d10 BI: DC 17 + 5.5 avg = **effective DC ~22-23**
- **COMBO:** Unsettling Words (bonus action) → Hypnotic Pattern/Hold Monster (action) = devastating

**Level 6 — Unfailing Inspiration:**
- When a creature uses your Bardic Inspiration and **fails** the roll, they **keep the die**
- Effectively makes your BI unlimited until someone succeeds
- Party members can keep trying with no waste

**Level 6 — Universal Speech:**
- Action: up to 5 creatures (CHA mod) within 60 ft understand you for 1 hour
- Doesn't require shared language
- Perfect for confronting foreign criminal organizations

**Level 14 — Infectious Inspiration:**
- When an ally uses your BI and **succeeds**, use your **reaction** to give BI to a different creature (no use expended)
- Combined with Unfailing: fail = keep it, succeed = it spreads to someone else
- Your Bardic Inspiration effectively **never runs out**

## Signature Combos

### 🎭 "The Reformation" (Signature Move)
*Rob a criminal, then rewrite their memory so they decided to reform.*

1. **Unsettling Words** (bonus action) — subtract d10 from target's next save
2. **Modify Memory** (action, 5th level) — Wis save at effective DC ~22-23
...

### 🧠 "The Mind Reader" (Social/Investigation)
*Tell them exactly what they want to hear.*

1. Cast **Detect Thoughts** before entering the room (lasts 1 minute).
2. Ask leading questions. "What's the biggest threat to your operation right now?"
3. Read their *surface thoughts* (No save required!). They will naturally think of the answer.
4. Adapt your con on the fly based on their fears and desires.
5. Use **Insight** to read physical tells and micro-expressions to corroborate the thoughts.
3. With **Instrument of the Bards** — target has **disadvantage** on the save (Modify Memory causes the Charmed condition)
4. Rewrite their memory: *"You realized your criminal empire was destroying lives. You gave your wealth to the poor and decided to seek honest work."*
5. Target genuinely believes this happened. At 6th+ level, modify memories up to a year old.

> **Success rate with all three layers (Unsettling Words + Instrument disadvantage + DC 17):**
> Even a target with +5 Wis save needs to roll ~17+ on disadvantage after subtracting d10. That's roughly **5-10% chance of success for them.** Your combo lands **90-95% of the time.**

### 🗡️ "The Shutdown" (Combat Opener)
*End an encounter before it starts.*

1. **Unsettling Words** (bonus action) on the biggest threat
2. **Hypnotic Pattern** (action, 3rd level) — 30 ft cube, Wis save
3. With **Instrument** — disadvantage on save (Hypnotic Pattern causes Charmed)
4. Most enemies fail. Incapacitated, speed 0.
5. Party mops up remaining enemies one at a time.

### 🕵️ "The Infiltration" (Social/Heist)
*Become someone else and walk right in.*

1. **Changeling Shapechanger** → become a known member of the criminal organization
2. **Observation** → rely on your high Insight/Perception to catch their mannerisms.
3. **Silver Tongue** → minimum 20+ Deception if questioned
4. Optionally: **Disguise Self** on top → add matching clothing/equipment illusion
5. Walk through the front door. You ARE that person.

### 🏃 "The Ghost" (Escape Plan)
*When things go sideways — vanish. We swapped Dimension Door for Polymorph and Greater Invisibility, so your escape relies on brute force biology or disappearing completely.*

1. **Greater Invisibility** → V, S. Keep fighting, buffing, or running away while totally unseen.
2. OR **Polymorph** → V, S, M. Cornered or Grappled? Turn into a **Giant Ape** (157 HP / +9 Athletics) to break out and crush the enemy, or a **Giant Eagle** (80 ft fly speed) to carry the loot out a window.
3. OR **Modify Memory** on witnesses → they never saw you
4. Shapeshift into someone completely different on the way out

### 💬 "The Sermon" (Post-Heist Persuasion)
*Convince the criminal they should be grateful.*

1. Silver Tongue ensures minimum 29 Persuasion
2. **Enhance Ability (Eagle's Splendor)** for advantage if stakes are high
3. Deliver a genuinely compelling argument about why crime doesn't pay
4. If they're resistant: **Suggestion** — *"Consider that everything I've said is true."*
5. If they're REALLY resistant: **Modify Memory** — make them remember having an epiphany

## Roleplaying Notes

### The Moral Compass
This character exists in a gray area. He's not a paladin — he steals, lies, and manipulates. But his targets are exclusively people who do worse. The interesting tension is:

- **When does "the ends justify the means" become its own evil?**
- **Is Modify Memory ethical if it genuinely reforms someone?** You're rewriting a person's identity without consent. That's terrifying.
- **What happens when he's wrong about a target?** What if someone he robbed was actually trying to go straight?

### Voice & Mannerisms
- Speaks with relaxed confidence — never rushed, never flustered
- Uses a LOT of names — remembers everyone's, uses them constantly (a con artist trick)
- When he's "preaching" to a criminal, his voice drops and becomes genuinely sincere — this is the part that's not an act
- His Changeling "true form" is rarely seen — even his allies may not know what he actually looks like

### Party Dynamics
- **To the Fighter:** "You hit things. I make sure the right things get hit."
- **To the Wizard:** "You read books about power. I use it."
- **To the Cleric:** Complicated — they might see through his moral compromises
- **To the Rogue:** Kindred spirits, but the Rogue may be more honest about what they are

- [ ] **Starting level** — locked at 8.
- [ ] **Feat Path (The Final Fork)** — We need to decide which "Grifter Archetype" to pursue for the Level 4 and Level 8 feats:
  - *Option A (The Mentalist):* Telepathic + Ritual Caster (The current baseline build). Uses innate mind-reading and a familiar for scouting.
  - *Option B (The Ghost):* Fey Touched (Misty Step) + Metamagic Adept (Subtle Spell). Casts social spells silently in front of people, impossible to lock down.
  - *Option C (The Wildcard):* Fey Touched (Misty Step) + Cartomancer. Trades undetectable casting for the ability to pull any highly-situational Bard spell (*Knock, Locate Object, Speak with Dead*) from a deck of cards once per day.
- [ ] **Magic Items** — We need 2 Uncommons. (Suggestions: Mac-Fuirmidh Cittern, Stone of Good Luck [Luckstone])
- [ ] **Criminal specialty** — Blackmailer? Burglar? Fence? Pickpocket? Highway robber? Smuggler?
- [ ] **The mentor** — who was the criminal who raised him? Are they still active?
- [ ] **Notable reformed criminals** — who are his "success stories"?
- [ ] **Multiclass consideration** — Pure Bard is strongest for this concept. But 1–2 Warlock levels (Archfey patron) could add: Eldritch Blast, 2 short-rest spell slots, Fey Presence (AoE charm/frighten). Worth discussing.
- [ ] **Fey type implications** — Does the DM rule Changeling as Fey (MotM) or Humanoid (ERLW)? This dramatically affects spell targeting.
