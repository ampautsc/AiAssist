NOTES
================================================================================

AC COLUMN
  Satyr        15 = Studded Leather + 3 Dex (CHA maxed, Dex secondary)
  Yuan-Ti      14 = Studded Leather + 2 Dex (slot spent on Resilient CON)
  Fairy        14 = Studded Leather + 2 Dex (slot spent on CHA +2)
  Halfling     14 = Studded Leather + 2 Dex (slot spent on CHA +2)
  Custom Lin   14 = Studded Leather + 2 Dex (War Caster + Telepathic + Broom)
  Tortle       18 = Natural Shell 17 + Cloak of Protection (dumps DEX entirely)
  Mtn Dwarf    17 = Half-Plate Medium Armor (free prof, no feat required)
  Gem Dragon   14 = Studded Leather + 2 Dex (innate telepathy, temp flight)
  Tiefling     14 = Studded Leather + 2 Dex (slot spent on CHA +2)

MAGIC RESISTANCE
  Satyr and Yuan-Ti are the only two species with Advantage on ALL spell saves.
  This makes them dramatically harder for enemy casters to control or kill.

FLIGHT
  Fairy        Permanent. No action, no concentration, no time limit.
  Custom Lin   Broom of Flying item. Limited uses but reliable.
  Gem Dragon   1 minute per short rest. Emergency only.

FEAT PATHS (Level 4 / Level 8)
  Satyr        Fey Touched (CHA 18, Misty Step, Silvery Barbs) / CHA +2 = 20
  Yuan-Ti      Resilient CON / CHA +2 = 20
  Fairy        Telekinetic (CHA 18, bonus shove) / CHA +2 = 20
  Halfling     Bountiful Luck / CHA +2 or Telepathic
  Custom Lin   Lvl1 Cartomancer / Lvl4 War Caster / Lvl8 Telepathic (3 feats)
  Tortle       Resilient CON / Telepathic
  Mtn Dwarf    Skill Expert (CHA 18) / CHA +2 = 20
  Gem Dragon   Metamagic Adept / Shadow Touched (CHA 18, Invisibility)
  Tiefling     Telepathic (CHA 18) / CHA +2 = 20

ADDITIONAL MAGICAL SECRETS (Level 6 - stolen from any class)
  Satyr        Counterspell + Pass Without Trace
  Yuan-Ti      Web + Spirit Guardians
  Fairy        Fireball + Find Steed
  Halfling     Healing Spirit + Spiritual Weapon
  Custom Lin   Counterspell + Revivify
  Tortle       Spirit Guardians + Shield
  Mtn Dwarf    Aura of Vitality + Crusaders Mantle
  Gem Dragon   Tashas Mind Whip + Thunder Step
  Tiefling     Pass Without Trace + Knock

STRENGTHS AND WEAKNESSES
  Satyr        BEST vs magic. No concentration protection, standard HP.
  Yuan-Ti      BEST poison/magic combo. No mobility options.
  Fairy        BEST mobility - permanent free flight. Standard defenses.
  Halfling     BEST team support (luck rerolls, hides behind allies). Slow/squishy.
  Custom Lin   BEST total package (everything via feats). No biological defenses.
  Tortle       BEST AC and durability. No flight, no magic resistance.
  Mtn Dwarf    BEST balanced defense (high AC free). Nothing exceptional.
  Gem Dragon   BEST free feat value (innate telepathy). Limited flight only.
  Tiefling     BEST infiltration (innate Invis + Disguise). Standard combat stats.
