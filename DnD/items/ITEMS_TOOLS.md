## Artisan's Tool Details

### Alchemist's Supplies ⭐⭐

**Components:** Two glass beakers, a metal frame to hold them, a glass stirring rod, a small mortar and pestle, a pouch of common alchemical ingredients

**Activities (XGtE p.79):**
| Activity | DC |
|----------|-----|
| Create a puff of thick smoke | 10 |
| Identify a poison | 10 |
| Identify a substance | 15 |
| Start a fire | 15 |
| Neutralize acid | 20 |

**Alchemical Crafting:** You can use alchemist's supplies to create:
- Acid (25 gp, 1 day)
- Alchemist's Fire (50 gp, 2 days)
- Antitoxin (50 gp, 2 days)
- Oil (10 cp, 1 hour)
- Perfume (5 gp, 4 hours)
- Soap (2 cp, 1 hour)

## Tool Details

### Disguise Kit ⭐⭐⭐

**Components:** Cosmetics, hair dye, small props, and a few different pieces of clothing

**Activities (XGtE p.81):**
| Activity | DC |
|----------|-----|
| Cover injuries or distinguishing marks | 10 |
| Spot a disguise being used by someone else | 15 |
| Copy a humanoid's appearance | 20 |

**Create Disguise:** Takes 1-10 minutes depending on complexity. Others make Wisdom (Insight) check vs your Charisma (Deception) check to see through it.

---

### Forgery Kit ⭐⭐

**Components:** Several different types of ink, a variety of parchments and papers, several quills, seals and sealing wax, gold and silver leaf, and small tools to sculpt melted wax

**Activities (XGtE p.81):**
| Activity | DC |
|----------|-----|
| Mimic 10 or fewer words of handwriting | 15 |
| Duplicate a wax seal | 20 |

**Forgery Detection:** When examining documents, add proficiency to Intelligence (Investigation) checks to detect forgeries.

---

### Herbalism Kit ⭐⭐

**Components:** Pouches to store herbs, clippers and leather gloves for collecting plants, a mortar and pestle, and several glass jars

**Activities (XGtE p.82):**
| Activity | DC |
|----------|-----|
| Find plants | 15 |
| Identify a plant | 15 |

**Create Antitoxin:** Given a day and proper materials, you can create an antitoxin (grants advantage on saves vs poison for 1 hour).

**Create Healing Potion:** You can spend 1 day and 25 gp to create a Potion of Healing.

---

### Navigator's Tools ⭐⭐

**Components:** A sextant, a compass, calipers, a ruler, parchment, ink, and a quill

**Activities (XGtE p.83):**
| Activity | DC |
|----------|-----|
| Plot a course | 10 |
| Discover your position on a nautical chart | 15 |

**Sighting:** Using navigator's tools, you can determine north and plot your ship's course.

---

### Poisoner's Kit ⭐⭐⭐

**Components:** Glass vials, a mortar and pestle, chemicals, and a glass stirring rod

**Activities (XGtE p.83):**
| Activity | DC |
|----------|-----|
| Spot a poisoned object | 10 |
| Determine the effects of a poison | 20 |

**Handle Poison:** Safely apply poison to weapons without risking exposure.

**Create Poison:** Use crafting rules (DMG p.258) to create poisons. See ITEMS_POTIONS.md for poison list.

---

### Thieves' Tools ⭐⭐⭐

**Components:** A small file, a set of lock picks, a small mirror mounted on a metal handle, a set of narrow-bladed scissors, and a pair of pliers

**Activities (XGtE p.84):**
| Activity | DC |
|----------|-----|
| Pick a lock | Varies (10-25 typically) |
| Disarm a trap | Varies |

**Pick Lock:** Add proficiency bonus to Dexterity checks to open locks.

**Disarm Trap:** Add proficiency bonus to Dexterity checks to disarm traps.
