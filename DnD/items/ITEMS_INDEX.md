## Quick Reference — Item Attunement

### Common Attunement Requirements

| Requirement | Examples |
|-------------|----------|
| **No Attunement** | Bag of Holding, Broom of Flying, +1/+2/+3 Weapons/Armor |
| **Yes (Any)** | Ring of Protection, Cloak of Displacement, Boots of Speed |
| **Yes (Spellcaster)** | Wand of Fireballs, Wand of the War Mage |
| **Yes (Bard)** | Instruments of the Bards, Rhythm-Maker's Drum |
| **Yes (Cleric/Druid/Paladin)** | Rod of Resurrection |
| **Yes (Warlock)** | Rod of the Pact Keeper |
| **Yes (Sorcerer/Warlock/Wizard)** | Staff of Power, Staff of the Magi |
| **Yes (Specific Class)** | Holy Avenger (Paladin), Staff of the Woodlands (Druid) |
| **Yes (Specific Alignment)** | Sword of Answering, Moonblade |
| **Yes (Specific Race)** | Whelm (Dwarf) |

## Quick Reference — Must-Have Items by Role

### Frontline Fighter/Barbarian/Paladin
- Belt of Giant Strength (any)
- Gauntlets of Ogre Power
- +1/+2/+3 Weapon
- Adamantine Armor
- Ring of Protection
- Cloak of Displacement

### Rogue/Ranger
- Boots of Elvenkind
- Cloak of Elvenkind
- Gloves of Thievery
- +1/+2/+3 Weapon (finesse or ranged)
- Bracers of Archery (Ranger)
- Ring of Invisibility

### Wizard/Sorcerer/Warlock
- Wand of the War Mage +1/+2/+3
- Rod of the Pact Keeper (Warlock)
- Staff of Power / Staff of the Magi
- Cloak of Protection
- Ring of Spell Storing
- Amulet of Proof Against Detection

### Cleric/Druid
- Staff of Healing
- Staff of the Woodlands (Druid)
- Amulet of Health
- Ring of Protection
- +1/+2/+3 Shield

### Bard
- Instrument of the Bards (any)
- Rhythm-Maker's Drum +1/+2/+3
- Reveler's Concertina
- Cloak of Elvenkind
- Ring of Spell Storing
