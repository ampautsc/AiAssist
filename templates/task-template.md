## Instructions

1. **Copy this template** when creating a new task
2. **Fill in all sections** completely
3. **Update Status and Last Updated** whenever you work on the task
4. **Check off items** in the Progress Checklist as completed
5. **Document blockers** as soon as they appear
6. **Update Notes** with important decisions or discoveries
7. **Move to archive** when Status becomes "Completed"

## Status Definitions

- **Not Started**: Task defined but no work begun
- **In Progress**: Actively working on the task
- **Blocked**: Cannot proceed due to dependency or issue
- **Completed**: All success criteria met and verified

## Priority Guidelines

- **High**: Urgent, blocking other work, or critical
- **Medium**: Important but not urgent
- **Low**: Nice to have, can be deferred

## Effort Estimates

- **Small**: < 2 hours
- **Medium**: 2-8 hours
- **Large**: 1-3 days
- **XL**: > 3 days (consider breaking down)
